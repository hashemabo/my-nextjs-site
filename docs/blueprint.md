# **App Name**: Elegance Boutique

## Core Features:

- Multi-Language Support: Supports both Arabic (RTL) and English, catering to the diverse user base in the Gulf region.
- Currency Conversion: Displays product prices in Saudi Riyal, Emirati Dirham, and Kuwaiti Dinar, providing convenience for customers in different Gulf countries.
- Culturally Relevant Imagery: Employs images that align with Gulf culture, representing luxury, exclusivity, and regional aesthetics.
- Product Display: Showcase 6-10 different products with high-quality images and prices in multiple Gulf currencies.
- Order Form Integration: A simplified order form is integrated with WhatsApp or Telegram for seamless order placement.
- Customer Review Highlighting: Display customer reviews and testimonials, giving preference to reviewers located in the Gulf region to increase user trust.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendations: An AI-powered tool recommends related products based on viewing history, the contents of the cart, and top seller lists, potentially increasing order value.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark Blue (#192A56), evoking trust and sophistication, in alignment with high-end tastes.
- Background color: Cream (#F5F5DC), offering a soft and elegant backdrop that complements the products.
- Accent color: Gold (#D4AF37), adding a touch of luxury and catching the user's attention.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a humanist sans-serif aligned to fashion and art.
- Body font: 'Alegreya', a serif font that matches the sophisticated design.
- Responsive design for optimal viewing on various devices.
- Use modern and minimalistic icons that are easy to understand.
